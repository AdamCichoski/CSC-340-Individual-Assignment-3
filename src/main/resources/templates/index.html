Our Mission

Our goal at Cassowary Concierge is to ensure that your cassowaries receive the best possible care in the process of learning to use a litter box.

Where To Schedule An Appointment

In the "Contact" tab on the left hand side, you can find contact information to schedule an appointment to start getting your cassowary litter box trained. Upon your request, one of our representatives will promptly reach out to you regarding your request and will send you any and all required documents to get us started on this journey.
